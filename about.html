About Me

Kit Zihlman has lived in Colorado all of their life. They are currently located in Fort Collins, where they are studying fullstack development.

When they aren't studying code, Kit is working a variety of front-facing customer service jobs. They have a diverse roster of experience, ranging from fast-paced bar employment in the downtown heart of their city to working at local wildlife shops.

Kit is always up for exploring the town and mountains, finding new craft beers, and talking about local birds. They have a backlog of video games years long to catch up on, and when they have time, they enjoy sewing, drawing and painting, and hanging out with their pet cat.
